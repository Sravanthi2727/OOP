GATE 2026 Exam Details

The Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering (GATE) is a national-level exam conducted by IISc and IITs for admission to postgraduate programs in engineering, technology, and science, and for PSU recruitment.

Mode: Online (Computer-Based Test)

Total Papers: 30+ (Based on Subjects)

Total Questions: 65

Total Marks: 100

Duration: 180 Minutes (3 Hours)

Key Dates for GATE 2026

Registration StartsAugust 28, 2025
Registration EndsOctober 13, 2025
Admit Card ReleaseJanuary 2, 2026
Exam DatesFebruary 7, 8, 14, 15, 2026
Results DeclarationMarch 19, 2026
