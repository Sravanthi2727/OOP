CEED 2026 Exam Details

The Common Entrance Examination for Design (CEED) is a national-level exam conducted by IIT Bombay for admission to Master of Design (M.Des) and Ph.D. programs in design at premier institutes like IITs and IISc. It evaluates creativity, visualization, design thinking, and analytical skills.

Mode: Part A: Computer-Based, Part B: Pen & Paper

Sections: Part A (Objective), Part B (Descriptive)

Total Marks: 200

Duration: 3 Hours

Key Dates for CEED 2025

Registration StartsOctober 1 2025
Registration EndsNovember 14 2025
Admit Card ReleaseJanuary 2 2026
Exam DateJanuary 18 2026 (Tentative)
Result DeclarationMarch 4 2026

🎨 Exam Pattern & Marking Scheme

Part Question Type Total Marks Duration
Part A Objective (MCQ, MSQ, NAT) 100 1 Hour
Part B Descriptive (Sketching, Design, Creativity) 100 2 Hours

*Only candidates who qualify Part A will have their Part B evaluated. Final CEED score is a weighted combination of both parts.
