GRE Exam Details

The Graduate Record Examination (GRE) is an international standardized test conducted by ETS, required for admission to many graduate and business schools worldwide. It assesses verbal reasoning, quantitative reasoning, and analytical writing.

Mode: Computer-Based / Home-Based

Sections: Verbal Reasoning, Quantitative Reasoning, Analytical Writing

Total Questions: 54 (varies slightly by test version)

Duration: ~1 hour 58 minutes

Key Dates for GRE 2025

Registration OpensAvailable Year-Round
Exam AvailabilityThroughout the Year (by Appointment)
Score Validity5 Years from Test Date
Result Time10–15 Days after Test

📝 Exam Pattern & Marking

Section Total Qs Time (Mins) Score Range
Analytical Writing 2 Tasks 30 0–6
Verbal Reasoning 27 41 130–170

*Scores for Verbal & Quantitative are reported separately; Analytical Writing is scored on a scale of 0–6.
